Course

English Classes

Our English classes are available to the entire Yangon community and not just matriculated students. There is a sliding scale for such courses depending upon one’s ability to pay. Matriculated students always get priority for classes. Conversation classes are limited to seven students maximum.

Office Business Classes

Office Business courses are available to the entire Yangon community. Priority placement is reserved for matriculated students. Costs are on a sliding scale depending upon the student’s ability to pay.


We teach the entire gamut of Microsoft Office Applications. We also offer English and Burmese typing classes.


Our state-of-the-art computer lab is available to students and alumni anytime during working hours, free-of-charge.

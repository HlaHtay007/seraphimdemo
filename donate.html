Those who would like to donate cash, goods and services to a Seraphim Learning Center or Seraphim College are asked to contact us at:

Both Seraphim College and Seraphim Learning Center are free to all Burmese citizens and anyone else who lives in Burma. We are a Catholic outreach to the poor of Burma who otherwise would have no access to a tertiary education and other, non-matriculated courses they might need to advance professionally in their field.

Those who wish to apply to Seraphim, should make an appointment to access their financial and academic needs.
